Avatar the Last Airbender Collage

Spring 2020

This is a mixed media collage made for a water media class. The piece is based on the hit television show and franchise, Avatar the Last Airbender (ALTA). In ATLA there are four nations that represent the four elements: fire, water, earth and air. Each nation contains some people with the ability to bend an element. The outside of the tri-fold is a non-representational approach, focused on each nation's bending abilities. The inside of the tri-fold is a representational approach., focused on the locations within the ALTA world. Below I have included parts of my design process.
