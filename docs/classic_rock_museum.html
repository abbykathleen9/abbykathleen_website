Classic Rock Museum

Spring 2020

The Classic Rock Museum is a fictitious museum. The Classic Rock Museum’s mission is to inspire, captivate, and educate all. The museum highlights famous rock artist from the 1960s to the 1980s, focusing on their stories and contributions to rock n’ roll. The mark, print materials, online applications, and merchandise use a vintage color palette and groovy design to pay tribute to the era of classic rock.
