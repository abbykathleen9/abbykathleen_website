Eau-Claire County Department
of Human Services Rebrand

Fall 2019

The designs shown are part of a group project I was a part of. Our team had the opportunity of collaborating together to work with and design for the Eau Claire County Department of Human Services. They are a city department that helps individuals and families in need to positively impact their lives while contributing to the well being of the community. Our team came in to help re-brand the department, reflecting what they do. As a team we created a new mark, posters, brochures, and hybrid pieces. We chose to use a color palette that would provide diversity and energy. We particularly chose saturated colors to create a welcoming feel.
