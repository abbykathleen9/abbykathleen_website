I'm Abby.

Profile

As a young child, I was always passionate about art. I attended a small high school where I was known as being an artistic student. I loved to draw, paint, and create collages. Throughout my life, my family has encouraged my artistic passions.

I always knew I wanted a career in the arts but was worried it was not a serious career choice. Within my first semester at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire, I changed my major several times, but always went back to art. I decided to pursue a career in Graphic Communications. I made myself become familiar with Adobe products and worked hard to become a better designer.

After diving into this major, I have appreciated graphic design has the possibility to use mixed medias, pull from different interests. As a designer, I love solving my client’s design problems. It is rewarding to see something I created on social media or on-air. It is so satisfying and humbling, especially to know others will see what I created.

Art is all around us and I will continue to contribute my designs to the world.

Design Experience

From Fall 2017 to Spring 2020 I have worked at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire's, Campus television station, TV-10. Since Fall 2018, I have been TV-10's Graphic Designer and Creative Director.

As Creative Director I lead a team of up to 25 TV-10 members. My job has included teaching each Creative Team member the basics of Adobe Premiere and how to use the station equipment, attending meetings, meeting with clients, and assigning projects to every member each week.

As their Graphic Designer, my main role is to create graphics for the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire’s athletic broadcasts shown on local television station, WEAU. I have also made printed and digital material for TV-10’s All-Nighter, 48 hour movie marathon. Elements including logo designs, posters, table tents, social media designs, Snapchat geofilters, attending weekly meetings, setting up film equipment, and team collaboration.

I have also be hired for freelance work througout my college career.

Skills

I am fluent in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Premiere. Proficient in Procreate and LiveText. In college, my course work included developing and designing brand identity, posters, brochures, websites, book designs, logos, community project, infographic design, and experience directly working with clients.
