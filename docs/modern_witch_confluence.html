Modern Witches Confluence

Fall 2019

The Modern Witches Confluence takes place annually in San Francisco, California. This project is a re-brand of the conference's print materials: brochure/poster, billboard, banner, book, and logo. Witchcraft can have archaic stereotypes tied to them. that are unrepresentative. The re-brand needed appeal to all types of witches' practices. The calm, clean color palette gives the conference an updated modern look.
