Posters

Fall 2018 - Spring 2020

This collection of posters were made for different clients. Wisconsin ArtsWest 41 was made for L.E. Philips Memorial Public Library. Empty bowls was created for Feed My People Food Bank. The CERCA poster and banner was designed for the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire's Office of Research and Sponsored Programs. The remaining posters and various digital materials were made to advertise the biannual 48 hour TV-10 All-Nighter.
